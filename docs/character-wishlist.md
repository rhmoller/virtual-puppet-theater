# 25 likely characters a kid might want to play

Companion to `prop-wishlist.md` — a list of personas a kid is likely
to ask for, with the prop combo that builds each one. Useful for:

- **Catalog presets** — anything a kid wants repeatedly is a candidate
  for a "character" macro that applies multiple cosmetics in one ask
  ("make me a pirate" → tricorn + eye patch + cutlass in a single
  turn).
- **Demo prompts** — hand a kid this list and let them pick a costume.
- **Asset-gen stress test** — many of these combine 3–4 cosmetics on
  the same puppet, exercising the slot system.

Each entry lists the prop combo (mostly drawn from `prop-wishlist.md`
or fresh asks for the generator).

## Adventure & classic

1. **Pirate** — tricorn hat, eye patch, cutlass in hand
2. **Knight** — knight's helmet, sword in hand, optional shield
3. **Cowboy / cowgirl** — cowboy hat, sheriff badge on neck, lasso or six-shooter
4. **Princess** — tiara, pearl necklace, magic wand
5. **King** / **Queen** — crown, royal scepter, gold medallion
6. **Sailor / sea captain** — sailor hat, telescope, pipe
7. **Viking warrior** — horned helmet, sword, bushy beard
8. **Soldier / explorer** — fire helmet (close enough), backpack, flashlight

## Magical & fantasy

9. **Wizard** — wizard hat, magic staff, long beard
10. **Witch** — witch hat, broom, black cat in arms
11. **Fairy** — flower crown, magic wand, butterfly wings
12. **Mermaid** — flower crown, seashell necklace, fish in hand
13. **Dragon** — dragon horns, breath-of-fire prop
14. **Unicorn** — single horn, flower mane

## Modern & everyday

15. **Firefighter** — fire helmet, hose, heroic stance
16. **Chef** — chef's hat, wooden spoon, apron-tie
17. **Doctor** — round glasses, stethoscope around neck, magnifying glass
18. **Astronaut** — astronaut helmet, antenna or flag in hand
19. **Police officer** — sheriff badge, walkie-talkie or baton
20. **Rock star** — sunglasses, microphone, lightning-bolt earrings
21. **Ballerina** — flower crown, single rose, tutu (collar approximation)

## Spooky & Halloween

22. **Vampire** — bat-shaped collar, fangs (dark cone teeth), red cape neck
23. **Ghost** — white sheet over head (half_sphere helmet white), no eyes
24. **Frankenstein's monster** — flat-top helmet, neck bolts (sphere on neck slot)

## Animal / creature

25. **Superhero** — bandit mask, cape (neck slot), lightning-bolt in hand
