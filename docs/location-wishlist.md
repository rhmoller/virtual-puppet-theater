# 20 likely locations a kid might want to visit

Companion to `prop-wishlist.md` — locations a kid is likely to ask
for, evoked by a small set of iconic scene props at named anchors.
The puppet theater never swaps the backdrop; a "location" is just a
distinctive combination of props placed at sky and ground anchors.

Each entry sketches the prop combo. Anchors available:
`sky_left`, `sky_center`, `sky_right`, `ground_left`, `ground_center`,
`ground_right`, `far_back`.

## Outdoor & nature

1. **Beach** — sun (sky), palm tree (ground_right), sand castle (ground_center), beach ball (ground_left)
2. **Forest** — clouds (sky), pine trees (ground_left & ground_right), mushroom (ground_center)
3. **Mountain peak** — sun (sky), mountain (far_back), rock or pine tree (ground_left)
4. **Desert** — sun (sky_center), cactus (ground_right), dune (ground_left)
5. **Arctic / North Pole** — full moon (sky), snowman (ground_center), igloo (ground_right), snowflakes (sky_left)
6. **Jungle** — sun (sky), palm trees (ground_left & right), monkey or vines (ground_center)
7. **Underwater / coral reef** — bubbles (sky), coral (ground_left), goldfish (ground_center), starfish (ground_right)
8. **Farm** — sun (sky), barn (far_back), pumpkin (ground_left), giant flower (ground_right)
9. **Magical garden** — rainbow (sky), giant flowers (ground_left & right), mushroom (ground_center)

## Sky & space

10. **Outer space / on the moon** — stars (sky_left & right), Earth (sky_center), crater (ground_center), rocket (ground_right)
11. **Above the clouds** — sun (sky_center), hot air balloon (sky_left), clouds (ground_left & right)
12. **Stormy sky** — dark cloud (sky_center), lightning bolts (sky_left & right), raindrops (ground anchors)

## Magical & fantastical

13. **Castle** — full moon (sky), castle tower (far_back), flag (sky_right), treasure chest (ground_center)
14. **Pirate ship at sea** — sun or moon (sky), sail mast (far_back), treasure chest (ground_center), cannon (ground_right)
15. **Dragon's lair / cave** — torch (sky_left), treasure chest (ground_center), dragon egg (ground_right), stalactites (sky_center)
16. **Fairy land** — rainbow (sky), giant mushrooms (ground_left & right), tiny fairy house (ground_center)

## Indoor & cozy

17. **Bedroom (bedtime)** — crescent moon (sky_right), star cluster (sky_left), pillow (ground_left), teddy bear (ground_center)
18. **Birthday party** — balloons (sky_left & right & center), cake (ground_center), party-hat banner (far_back)

## Other special

19. **Carnival / fair** — sun (sky), Ferris wheel (far_back), striped tent (ground_left), balloons (sky_right)
20. **Concert stage** — spotlight (sky_center), microphone stand (ground_center), speaker (ground_left), guitar case (ground_right)
